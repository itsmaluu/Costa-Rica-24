Wildlife Gallery

Resplendent Quetzal - A beautiful bird found in Costa Rica's cloud forests. (Pharomachrus mocinno)
American Crocodile - Spotted in the rivers and swamps of Costa Rica. (Crocodylus acutus)
Leafcutter Ant - Famous for their impressive leaf-cutting skills. (Atta cephalotes)
Blue Morpho Butterfly - Known for its stunning blue wings. (Morpho menelaus)
Brown Basilisk - Also known as the 'Jesus Christ lizard' for its ability to run on water. (Basiliscus vittatus)
Cane Toad - An invasive species with a voracious appetite. (Rhinella marina)
Red-Eyed Tree Frog - Iconic for its bright red eyes and colorful body. (Agalychnis callidryas)
Glass Frog - Known for its translucent skin. (Centrolenidae)
Green and Black Poison Dart Frog - A small but strikingly beautiful frog. (Dendrobates auratus)
Toucan - Recognizable by its large, colorful bill. (Ramphastos toco)
Hermit Hummingbird - Tiny and fast, often seen hovering near flowers. (Phaethornithinae)
Scarlet-Rumped Cacique - Known for its bright red rump. (Ramphocelus passerinii)
Howler Monkey - Famous for its loud howls that can be heard for miles. (Alouatta)
White-Faced Capuchin Monkey - A highly intelligent and curious monkey.
Spider Monkey - Known for its long limbs and prehensile tail.
